Calc II - 2023

Course notes for a condensed second course in university calculus that I taught in the Summer B 2023 Semester at Dalhousie. These were designed to be read as a workbook covering select parts of the official textbook (the second half of Stewart's Calculus 12th edition), I would fill them in and annotate by hand in lecture and post both versions before and after class respectively along with recordings.
